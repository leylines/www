About

Leylines are alignments of ancient sites or holy places, such as stone circles, standing stones and churches. leylines.ch archives and researches lines and sites and tries to find a global network of energy-lines which interconnects the local leylines to a big energynet.

The main part of the website is a browser based 3D-globe for the research and visualisation of sites and alignments.

leylines.ch is a non commercial site. The purpose of this site is the preservation, enhancement and research of old and new knowledge about global leylines.
